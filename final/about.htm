Mission Statement

Subspace music aims to bring history, knowledge, and archival to interested and good willing people. Our mission is to preserve and maintain musical information and deliver it in a presentable way to the masses. We will hold a great respect for the artists and their rights as artists. We will secure and privitize and anonymize all relevant information of our users.

Goals

  • Deliver presentable information
  • Archive musical information
  • Respect artist's rights
  • Secure and anonymize user information
  • Evade the use and gathering of user information

Data Collection

Here at Subspace Music, We strive to collect the least amount of user data. It is our philosophy that collecting user data is bad and should not be done. If you have an account, The only data we collect is your username, password and edit history.

Artist's Rights

We at Subspace Music will amend our site towards any artist's rights or requests. If the artists wishes we remove, edit anything on their page, we will do so in a timely manner.

Archival

At Subspace Music, we try to archive musical information because, alike our users, we love music and wish to mark and maintain historic and valuable information to preserve artist's lineage and achievements.
